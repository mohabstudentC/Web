Introduction

Koshary is a national dish and one of Cairo's most famous street foods. It has a base of rice and macaroni and is mixed with lentils or chickpeas. Vinegar and tomato sauce are added to it. It is then garnished with fried onions. It is a simple traditional food that is considered to be vegan heaven.
